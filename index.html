Hello, this is Xiaoyu YANG (杨小羽), or you can call me Derick. I work at the intersections of human-computer interaction, information policy, and social computing.

About

I am a first-year PhD student in Information and Media at Michigan State University, advised by Morgan Vigil-Hayes, and a member of the LOOM Research and Design Lab. I have completed my MPhil in Computational Media & Arts at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), where I was advised by Pan HUI and was a member of MC2, the center for metaverse and computational creativity. I love playing the guitar and doing Chinese calligraphy.
